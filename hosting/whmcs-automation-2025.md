## What Is WHMCS?

WHMCS is an all-in-one client management, billing, and support system built for hosting businesses, resellers, and web agencies.

Think of it as the business backbone that sits on top of your hosting stack (cPanel, Plesk, DirectAdmin, VPS, domains) and automates the "boring but critical" part:

* **Invoicing & Payments**
* **Provisioning hosting accounts**
* **Domain registration & renewals**
* **Support tickets & knowledgebase**

In other words: WHMCS helps you get paid on time, keep clients happy, and scale without drowning in manual tasks.

## Key Features of WHMCS in 2025

### 1. Automated Billing & Invoicing
Generate invoices automatically (monthly, yearly, custom). It integrates with 75+ payment gateways including PayPal, Stripe, and even Crypto. It also handles taxes, VAT, and sends automatic reminders for overdue invoices.

### 2. Hosting Account Provisioning
The moment a client pays, WHMCS can automatically create their account on cPanel, Plesk, or DirectAdmin. If they stop paying? The system automatically suspends them.

### 3. Domain Management
Fully automated domain search, registration, transfer, and renewal. It integrates with major registrars like GoDaddy, Namecheap, and ResellerClub.

### 4. Client Area & Self-Service Portal
Clients get their own login where they can manage services, pay invoices, and open support tickets. This significantly reduces your manual workload.

### 5. Support Desk Integration
A professional ticketing system with departments (billing, technical, sales), SLA timers, and canned responses.
